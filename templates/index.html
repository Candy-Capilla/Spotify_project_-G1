Music (DATA) Mixer

Inspired by DJs mixing the top hits at the bar, the Music (DATA) Mixer let's you create your own mix tape, but with data.
Using data from the Spotify API, take a look at the data behind your favorite years of music.

Total Dataset

41,450 Songs

Sum of Song Lengths

3.65 Months

Created By

Max Browning, Candy Capilla, Essi Monneus

Variables

Tempo: The speed at which a passage of music is or should be played.

Speechiness: The presence of spoken or sung words in a track.

Popularity: The state of being liked or supported by many people.

Loudness: The subjective perception of sound pressure.

Instrumentalness : The quality or condition of being instrumental.

Energy: The combination of volume, pitch, and frequency.

Danceability: Appropriate for or conducive to dancing.

Explicitness: Containing graphic or offensive language.


* All variables have been standardized to range from 0 - 100.
